Tamagotchi Game

Fun Fact: 1st Game & 1st Project

Experience the nostalgic 90's game, Tamagotchi, with a modern twist! Introducing the 2023 version of virtual Tamagotchi, an interactive web app developed using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Begin by naming your adorable pet and embark on an exciting journey of virtual pet care. Your pet's well-being depends on you!

You'll find three essential buttons to keep your virtual baby alive and happy. Click each button strategically to prevent the levels from dropping by 10%. Remember to provide nourishing food, ensure your baby gets enough sleep, and engage in entertaining activities. Let your inner child rejoice and enjoy the delightful experience of caring for your virtual Tamagotchi baby. Have a blast!

Dide's Kitchen

Fun Fact: 1st Website

Introducing my recipe storage application, a comprehensive virtual recipe book that leverages the power of the 7 RESTful routes and CRUD functionality. Built using Node.js, Express, Mongoose, and EJS, this full-stack application is designed to organize and display all your recipes seamlessly.

With this app, you can easily store and manage your own recipes in a dedicated tab. The user-friendly interface allows you to add new recipes with ease, complete with captivating images to enhance the visual experience. Once you've added your recipes, they will be showcased under the "Guest Recipes" section, providing a platform for sharing and exploring culinary creations with other users. Get ready to unlock your culinary creativity with this dynamic recipe storage app.
